How it works

Three steps. Zero effort.

Babushka works silently in the background — your family browses normally and only sees a warning when something is actually wrong.

Step 01
🧩
Install the extension
One click from the Chrome Web Store. No account, no sign-up, no data collected. Add your own Claude API key in the settings — yours stays yours.
Step 02
🌐
Browse normally
Babushka watches quietly. It only activates when it detects a payment or checkout form — or a suspicious email in Gmail. You won't notice it's there until you need it.
Step 03
🛡️
Get warned before you pay
Babushka reads all the terms, fine print, and renewal clauses — then shows a plain-English summary right next to the payment form. No jargon. No confusion. Just the truth.
